About

The destination may be comfy, but the journey is not...

1964-1971

Humble Beginnings

Grandpa Joseph realized that there are no outdoor stores in his hometown. He thought, "I'll do it myself."

May 7, 1972

First Store Opening

It may have took Grandpa Joseph eight years to set up shop, but that didn't stop him from actualizing Comfy Hut.

1973-1980

Expansion

Since people loved Grandpa Joseph's store, he decided to set up branches throughout the state, so more folks could enjoy the great outdoors!

1981-Present

Going Global

Wanting to share his love of the outdoors, Grandpa Joseph decided to expand further throughout America and the world.

Founders

Three avid campers making everyone else's outdoor experiences comfy!
